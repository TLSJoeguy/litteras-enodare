# L.ENODARE
### litteras enodare
*latin*
/lit.tɛ.ras ɛ.noˈdaː.rɛ/
"making clear the letters"; phrase meaning "to decipher" or "to explain letters"; the act of reading a message, untangling obscure written texts, or explaining the precise meaning of words and documents.

This is just a "home base" for resources and ideas relating to my cyberdeck project, which is centered around analyzing serial communication protocols, radio transmissions, as well as kind of being an art project.
I just thought the name was cool in an eleven-year-old-ish sort of way.
